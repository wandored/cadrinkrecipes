```{note}

```

```{list-table}
:header-rows:  1
* - Ingredients
  - Amount
* - Tito's Vodka
  - 1-1/2 oz
* - Pampelmousse Liquer
  - 1/2 oz
* - Lemon Juice
  - 3/4 oz
* - Grapefruit Juice
  - 1 oz
* - Simple Syrup
  - 1/2 oz
```

## PROCEDURE
1. Add to shaker with ice
2. Hard shake for 15 seconds
3. Strain into a rocks glass with ice
4. Garnish with a lemon peel
