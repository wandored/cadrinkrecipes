```{list-table}
:header-rows:  1
* - Ingredients
  - Amount
* - Ford's Gin
  - 1-1/2 oz
* - Green Chartreuse
  - 3/4 oz
* - Lime Juice
  - 3/4 oz
* - Simple Syrup
  - 3/4 oz
* - Egg White
  - 1/2 oz
```

## PROCEDURE
1. Build in a shaker and dry shake for 5 seconds
2. Add ice
3. Hard shake for 15 seconds
4. Strain into a coupe
5. Garnish with a luxardo cherry
