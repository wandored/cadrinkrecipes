```{image} ../images/blueHawaiian.jpg
:alt: blue_hawaiian
```

:::{Notes}
GLASS: Hurricane
GARNISH: Pineapple & Cherry
:::

## INGREDIENTS
* 1-1/2 oz  Bacardi
* 3/4 oz    Blue Curacao
* 2 oz      Pineapple Juice
* 1/4 oz    Coco Lopez
* 1/2 oz    Lemon Juice

### PROCEDURE
1. Build all ingredients in shaker with ice
2. Shake for 15 seconds
3. Pour into iced hurricane glass
4. Garnish with pineapple and cherry

#### SECRETS OF SUCCESS
* Bright yellow pineapple
* Fresh cherry
