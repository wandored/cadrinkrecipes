## INGREDIENTS
* 1-1/2 oz  Jim BeamPeach
* 1/2 oz    DeKuyper Peachtree
* 2 oz      Unsweet Tea
* 2 oz      Lemonade

## PROCEDURE
1. Combine in tim with ice
2. Shake until tin is frosty
3. Pour over fresh ice
4. Garnish with lemon slice

:::{SECRETS OF SUCCESS}
* Fresh Garnish
:::
