# Woo Woo

```{image} ../../images/woowoo.png
:alt: woowoo
```

```{note}
* GLASS: Martini
* GARNISH: Lime strip
```

```{list-table}
:header-rows:  1
* - Ingredients
  - Amount
* - Absolut
  - 1 oz
* - Peach Schnapps
  - 1 oz
* - Cranberry Juice
  - 2 oz
```

## PROCEDURE
1. Add all ingredients to shaker with ice
2. shake until frost forms on tin
3. Strain into Martini glass

```{important}
* Curly strip of lime
* chill glass with ice
```
