# Coco Loco

```{image} ../../images/cocoLoco.jpg
:alt: coco_loco
```

```{note}
* GLASS: Rocks
* GARNISH: Pineapple Wedge
```

```{list-table}
:header-rows:  1
* - Ingredients
  - Amount
* - Patron Silver
  - 1-1/2 oz
* - Midori
  - 1/2 oz
* - Fresh Lime Juice
  - 1 oz
* - Coco Lopez
  - 1 oz
* - Pineapple Juice
  - 2 oz
* - Ginger Beer
  - Fill
```

## PROCEDURE
1. Add all ingredients to shaker tin and shake til frosty
2. Strain over fresh ice
3. Garnish with pineapple wedge

```{important}
* Bright yellow pineapple
```
