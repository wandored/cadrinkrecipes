# Smokin' Paloma

```{image} ../images/
:alt: 
```

:::{note}
GLASS: Highball
GARNISH: Grapefruit wedge
:::

## INGREDIENTS
* 2 oz      Bahnez Mezcal
* 1 oz      Grapefruit Juice
* 1/2 oz    Simple Syrup
* 1/4 oz    Lime Juice
* Splash    Club Soda

### PROCEDURE
1. Build in a shaker
2. Add ice
3. Shake for 15 seconds
4. Strain over ice in a highball glass
5. Garnish with a grapefruit wedge

#### BACKGROUND NOTES
* Mezcal adds a smokiness to the traditional Paloma
