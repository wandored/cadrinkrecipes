# Greenback

```{image} ../images/
:alt: 
```

* GLASS: Coupe
* GARNISH: Luxardo cherry

## INGREDIENTS
* 1-1/2 oz  Ford's Gin
* 3/4 oz    Green Chartreuse
* 3/4 oz    Lime Juice
* 3/4 oz    Simple Syrup
* 1/2 oz    Egg White

### PROCEDURE
1. Build in a shaker and dry shake for 5 seconds
2. Add ice
3. Hard shake for 15 seconds
4. Strain into a coupe
5. Garnish with a luxardo cherry
