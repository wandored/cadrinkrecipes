## INGREDIENTS
* 2 oz  Johnnie Walker Red
* 1 oz  Luxardo Amaretto

### PROCEDURE
1. Build in a rocks glass
2. Add a large ice cube
3. Garnish with an orange peel# SECRETS OF SUCCESS

#### BACKGROUND NOTES
* Great pre or post dinner cocktail
* Smokey and sweet
