# COCO LOCO

## INGREDIENTS
* 1-1/2 oz  Patron Silver
* 1/2 oz    Midori
* 1 oz      Fresh Lime Juice
* 1 oz      Coco Lopez
* 2 oz      Pineapple Juice
* Fill      Ginger Beer

## PROCEDURE
1. Add all ingredients to shaker tin and shake til frosty
2. Strain over fresh ice
3. Garnish with pineapple wedge

### SECRETS OF SUCCESS
* Fresh ingredients
