# CUCUMBER SMASH

## INGREDIENTS
* 2 oz  Grey Goose Vodka
* 1 oz  Lime Juice
* 1 oz  Simple syrup
* 10    Mint Leaves
* 4     Cucumber Wheels
* Fill  Ginger Beer

## PROCEDURE
1. Add all ingredients, except ginger beer to shaker tin and muddle
2. Add ice and shake
3. Strain over fresh ice
4. Garnish with cucumber wheel and mint
